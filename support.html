Help & contact information

FretShed is a guitar practice app that generates unique solos for ear training. There are no tabs - just you, your guitar, and your ear. Designed for intermediate to advanced players who want to sharpen their listening skills and break out of the patterns they already know.

We're actively developing new features and refining the experience based on player feedback. Some things may change, and we appreciate your patience as we build.

Frequently Asked Questions

What genres does FretShed support?

FretShed currently generates solos in five genres: rock, blues, jazz, metal, and funk. Additional genres are planned for future updates.

How does A-B looping work?

Tap the A marker at the start of a phrase you want to focus on, then tap the B marker at the end. FretShed will loop that section continuously so you can work on it until you have it. Tap either marker again to clear it.

Can I slow down a solo without changing the pitch?

Yes. The speed control lets you reduce playback speed to as low as 50% while keeping the pitch intact. Once you've got a phrase at slow tempo, increase the speed gradually until you can play it at full tempo.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad and select FretShed to cancel. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select your FretShed purchase to submit a refund request.

Are the solos truly unique each time?

Each solo is generated by AI and will be different every time you hit generate. You'll never be given the same material twice, which is the whole point — your ear needs fresh, unfamiliar material to grow.

Is FretShed available on Mac?

FretShed is currently available on iPhone and iPad. Mac support is in development and coming soon.

Does FretShed require an internet connection?

An internet connection is required to generate solos, as the AI processing happens on our servers. Once a solo has been generated and downloaded to your device, you can practice with it offline.
